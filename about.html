Our Story

Founded in 2020 in Cape Town, Bean Theory is an artisan coffee roastery sourcing ethically grown beans from African farms.

Our Journey

Bean Theory began with a simple passion for exceptional coffee and a commitment to supporting African coffee farmers. What started as a small roastery in Cape Town has grown into a beloved brand known for quality and ethical sourcing.

Mission

"To roast the freshest, most traceable coffee while supporting African farmers."

Vision

"Become South Africa's most trusted craft coffee brand."
